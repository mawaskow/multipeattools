European Peatland Map Data

Our European peatland map is an approximation of the peat soils of Europe based on various types of maps across European Union member state countries and the United Kingdom. Not every country has a national peatlands or peat soils map, and even when they do, definitions of peatlands vary across countries, as well as the format of their data. When countries have no dedicated map of national peatlands, we estimate peat soils from maps of organic soils or ecological contexts, topographic surveys, and other related fields. Additionally, we use the European Wetland Map which similarly has created approximate maps of national wetlands across Europe based on various types of data. We filter layers from this dataset to only include "Peatlands."
